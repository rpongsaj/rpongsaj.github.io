Approach

Bring people together

As product manager, I bridge the design and development processes on my team, helping to refine design concepts and turn them into actionable development tasks. I also communicate regularly with audiences, stakeholders, and project managers to better understand their needs and to ensure that our work is timely and grounded in reality.

Build sites for humans

It's important to me that people connect with the sites that I help build—not just technically but emotionally. I've had the privilege of sharing my design & development skills and general enthusiasm for the web with colleagues and regularly work with students and faculty on academic and professional portfolios.

Continually improve

At Georgetown, my overarching goal has been to improve the many websites we help maintain. I've helped define our web strategy and have been focused on making regular improvements that add up to a significantly better experience in the long run—and championing our vision both internally and to the rest of the university.

Work

Shifting web platforms at Georgetown

In 2018, Georgetown made the decision to switch from Drupal to WordPress—and to migrate 300+ sites to the new platform within two years. I led the initial planning and oversaw design and development of our new WordPress theme—built to take full advantage of the new Gutenberg editor. I also worked with three project managers to direct our communications effort to content editors at the university.

Digital pedagogy and working with faculty

I worked closely with faculty in my previous position at CNDLS, helping them design and implement technology-enhanced learning experiments ranging from creating digital microscopy exhibits to integrating Wikipedia editing into their courses. I also managed large datasets from Georgetown's massive open online courses (MOOCs), performing analyses and creating reports and visualizations.

Mentorship, teaching, and presentations

I've presented sessions on information architecture, writing for the web, user-centered design, and learning analytics to various audiences over the years. I also supervised the work of several graduate students at CNDLS and have advised 150+ Georgetown graduate and undergraduate students on their professionally-focused web portfolios.
